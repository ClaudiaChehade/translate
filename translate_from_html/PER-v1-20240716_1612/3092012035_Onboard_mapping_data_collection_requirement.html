Data TAG demand

Tag type

  1. City: such as Suzhou, Shanghai, etc.

  2. Parking lot description: For example, a mall, a community, etc.Large or small parking lots.And the new and old level of parking lot facilities.

  3. External environment description: indoor or outdoor, during the day or night.For example, the indoor day, outdoors at night.And the description of the light situation.

  4. Other tags: entrance, number of layers, time to enter and drive out of the parking lot.
