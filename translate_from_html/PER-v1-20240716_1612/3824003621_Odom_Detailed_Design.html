three.Dynamic design


3.1 ODOM status machine diagram


Odomprovider's status machine has two states: Perody_oninit And polodom_ONUPDATE.When the system is turned on, first enter the Perodom_oninit state. If the initialization is successful, enter the Perodom_ONUPDATE state. After entering Perodom_ONUPDATE, Odomhandler receives correlation data per 10ms for processing and then sends the calculation results.

The sequential charts and flow charts in the following are different from the two different states of Perodom_oninit and Perodom_ONUPDATE, respectively.
