Environmental requirements

4S store

  • Avoid direct sunlight, good lighting

  • Flat flat (the flatness of the vehicle parking area is <1mm, the inclinedness is <1 °), the solid color matte (recommended for epoxy self -leveling process), and there is no debris in the calibration area

  • The vehicle hangs N gear and use the tire fixed device to prevent movement

Open road (triggering dynamic calibration in special circumstances)

  • Good light conditions, avoiding rain and snow, foggy weather, avoiding the morning and evening peaks and other congestion periods

  • Three lanes and above, clear lane lines, there are buildings around and rich in texture
