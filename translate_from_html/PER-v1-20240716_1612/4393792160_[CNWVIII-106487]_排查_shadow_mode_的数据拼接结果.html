[CNWVIII-106487] Check the data stitching result of Shadow Mode


CNWVIII-106487 - Jira issue doesn't exist or you don't have permission to view it.

in conclusion

1. The Parking data recorded by shadow_mode has the following problems

  • Most data packets 4 -way environmental camera images are incomplete

  • All data lacks offline and online calibration parameters

  • Mixed with a small amount of Driving scene data (such as elevated)

2. Has been developed and provided related Tools, see for details View Camera BEV Stitching -Data Analysis Guide -Compatible with Shadow_mode, Parking, Line calibration and after -sales calibration data processing The main steps and function descriptions are as follows (VAL is responsible for deploying the tool to compliance cloud)

  • Shadow_mode data pre -processing (whether the check packet is valid, search DUMP data according to VIN, statistical timestamp information of the same car of the same car, and query results of the calibration parameter)

  • Extract the original image and calibration parameter from Rosbag

  • Based on the timestamp of the front -view camera, synchronize the 4 -way surround camera image to generate effective frames (record the original timestamp and difference, and check whether the splicing effect is not good due to different splicing effects)

  • Generate BEV stitching related files according to the calibration parameter

  • Batch bev stitching

3. The existing data is not enough to compare the BEV puzzle of the same car at different times to determine whether the external parameters will change (when Val analyzes more client Parking, the shadow_mode data)

4. The calibration parameter in UUID is not taken from the latest DUMP. The same car is multiple times When the production line is calibrated or after -sales calibration, the INTRINSIC Update is 3, and the internal parameters are not updated and the internal and external participation is not worthy

Intrinsic Update (Find UUID first, multiple parameters retain the latest, and you can't find the local DUMP)

  • 0: UUID is not found, and DUMP is not found in the local area (unable to update, inside and outside the outside and outside are not available)

  • 1: UUID is not found, and found DUMP locally, but the files are damaged (unable to update, uncomfortable internal and external participation)

  • 2: Internal and external participation in uUID or DUMP participation in Persistency is completely consistent (no need to update, internal and external participation facilities)

  • 3: Except for CX and CY, there are other inconsistencies in the internal and external Persistency in UUID or DUMP. It is possible that the production line will be calibrated again, which is not consistent with the historical history in UUID (not updated, inside and outside are not available)

  • 4: The update is successful, and the inside and outside participation facilities
