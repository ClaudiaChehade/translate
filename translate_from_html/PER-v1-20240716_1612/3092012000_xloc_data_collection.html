Data TAG demand

The attributes that TAG cares about, the principle is to classify the attributes in the scene classification in 3.3. One column of one attribute, one data often meets multiple attributes, and has corresponding attributes.

  1. Level first tag: city

  2. Second TAG: Classification of the route (high -speed, viaduct, city fast, main roads, secondary roads, branch roads, etc.)

  3. Three TAG: Regional classification (internal and foreign ring, open, tunnel, cover, etc.)

  4. Level 4 TAG: Special Region Classification (Long Tunnel, Bridge, etc.)

  5. Level 5: Climate (winter during winter, rainy days in winter, etc.)
