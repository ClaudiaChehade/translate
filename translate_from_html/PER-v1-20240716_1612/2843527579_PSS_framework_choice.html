Parking space construction strategy

Rules -based algorithm Front fusion : Model the relative location relationship of different obstacles and peripheral obstacles as different preset scenarios. Select the current environment that the current environment is the most consistent with the decision tree, so as to generate ExternalPS

Rules -based algorithm Rear fusion : From UPER and VIPER to build space parking spaces and line parking spaces, and then combine or integrate space parking spaces and line parking spaces to obtain ExternalPS
