OTA3 Online Calibration Disturb Test

Reference document Online Calibration test requirement & data analysis

background:

1. The external parameter of Camera yaw pitch roll to verify whether the optimization result of the online calibration is consistent with the disturbance value to ensure the function

2. Publicization test should Fully cover the following CORNER CASE

Road conditions

The confidence of the 2D lane detection can be used as an objective standard

good

  • High -speed, elevated, urban fast roads (flat roads)

  • The three lanes live in the middle, and the lane is clear

  • Non -congestion period

bad

  • General urban roads (bumpy roads)

  • Below three lanes (parking lots), The Lane Line is Not Clear

  • Morning and evening peaks, congestion periods (there are many surrounding vehicles, lane lines are blocked)

Weather conditions

good

  • Sunny and cloudy days

bad

  • At night, rainy days (small/medium/large), heavy fog, backlight

Driving habit

good

  • The speed 40 ~ 100 km/h

  • Driving smoothly, try to go straight to avoid changing lanes

bad

  • Too low or too high

  • Frequent changes 、 Accelerate 、 Acute deceleration 、 Sudden brake 、 Turn a sharp turn

Option 1: Camera external parameters are truly changed through mechanical devices

advantage:

  • True and credible

  • No need to modify the lane line extraction and online calibration related software

shortcoming:

  • It is necessary to design and process machinery devices. The design and production cycle is long, and the modification of vehicles is difficult.

  • Unable to comprehensively cover special scenarios, Road Conditions and specific weather, light conditions, etc.

  • Effective data screening difficulty

  • Wasting real car test resources

Option 2: Use Synthetic Data Generation to provide simulation data

advantage:

  • You can simulate all Camera and lidar

  • Can be generalized to get rich Corner Case data, improve the online calibration performance

  • Can provide closed -loop testing, greatly reduce test costs

  • For the test environment in dangerous scenes, not only can reduce the cost of scene construction, but also reduce the safety risks of testers

shortcoming:

  • There are differences with the real environment

  • Need to adapt software, the current output is a single -frame image and the real value of the 3D lane line, not Rosbag

    • How to perceive the lane line and enter the online calibration module?(Temporarily convert the true value of the 3D lane line through the external parameter to the image)

    • How to initialize the production line calibration parameters?

    • How to update the online calibration results?

    • How to evaluate the impact on function?
