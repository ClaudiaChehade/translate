Precautions

1. Daily Build test based on 20240314

2. Confirm that the production line calibration parameters of the sensor are normal

3. Check whether the installation of the camera is stable and whether the lens is clean

4. Drive smoothly, do not accelerate, slow down, rapid brakes and rapid turns

5. Check whether the output of viper_lane and local_loc is normal. For example

6. Normal driving for more than 20 min, speed 40 ~ 100 km/h

7. The following scene is not suitable for online monitoring and online calibration

  • Morning and evening peaks, vehicles are congested before and after

  • Rain and snow, heavy fog, night, etc.

  • Non -urban road conditions, the roads in front of the vehicle are uneven, and the bumps are obvious

  • PARKING LOT

  • The lane line is not clear

Verification scheme

Positive test

Apply the following disturbance to YAW of the front -view camera:

(Camera disturbance parameter file generate script: disturb_calib_param.py，

How to use: python disturb_calib_param.py --Folder_path = [XXTRINSRC_FOLDER] -Sensor_name = [Sensor_name]

[Optional] -Delta_yaw = 0.0 -Delta_pitch = 0.0 -Delta_roll = 0.0)

(Note: When the t_v_c in the calibration parameter is rotated in order of Euler XYZ, the pitch angle under the vehicle department corresponds to the ROLL here, so the following pitch disturbance needs to be added to the DELTA_ROLL)
